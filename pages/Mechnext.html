Mechnext

Team mechnext is one of the six prominent technical clubs of J.C bose university of science and technology ymca eshtablished in January 2008. It is one of the core technical club's of ymca providing practical knowledge to the students regarding mechanical engineering and covering other feild aspects also ,its aim is to nourish the knowledge with right practical work and innovation.

About us

Mechnext is one of the 6 prominient technical clubs of J C Bose University. Our motive and mission is to integrate mechanical, electronic and intelligent control sub-systems & uniting students in a multidisciplinary environment. The Employability of design principles, processes, models, tool sets and philosophy unique to Mechatronic engineers enables design and development of simpler, more economical and reliable systems.

Our Motto

Image by Marvin Meyer The mission of Mechnext Club is to unite students in an educational environment and provide students a place to pursue their interest in mechanical, electronic, and software design by working on large and small scale projects. However, the club provides much more than just technical growth to its members, from team-building to cooperation, from perseverance to sincerity, the club teaches you a myriad of interpersonal skills essential in daily life. The club is open to students with or without experience because, believe it or not, none of us had any idea of project when we first stepped into the club room, neither do we expect anything from you. You just need to show up and the club and its people will take care of the rest. The club's environment perpetuates the sharing of wisdom and experience amongst our members. All we need from you is enthusiasm!
