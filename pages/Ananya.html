Ananya

Ananya is the literary and debating society of Jc Bose University of Science and Technology, Faridabad. Here at Ananya, a bunch of people with liberal minds try to contribute towards bringing change to the society by the medium of our speeches, poems and stories. At Ananya, we regularly organise sessions of debate, group discussions, creative writing, turncoat etc which are open for everyone to participate. We also organise a major event like YMCA Youth Parliament, Kaavyotsav etc every single year where we observe a massive turnout of students within Delhi-NCR as well as outside of it.
