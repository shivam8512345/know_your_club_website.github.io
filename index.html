All About Ymca university

J.C. Bose University was established in 1969 as an Indo-German project, a joint venture of the National Council of YMCAs of India, the Government of Haryana and the German Central Agencies for Development Aid, Bonn, Germany. It was affiliated to M.D. University In 1996 the state government took full control over the college. In 2009 it became a university through the YMCA University of Science and Technology Faridabad Act, 2009. In 2018 it was renamed to its current name through the YMCA University of Science and Technology Faridabad (Amendment) Act, 2018.

Cultural activities

Manan is the computer society of J.C Bose University. We aim to garner creativity and talent & propagate awareness regarding various tech-related knowledge throughout the ingenious minds.

Nataraja, The Dancing Society of JCBUST, was started for the students of jcbust to overcome the inhibition of public stage performances.Nataraja comprises of three dance forms- bhangra,western and haryanvi.

Sports activities keep the body fit and mind agile. National School of Business offers facilities for indoor and outdoor sports such as chess, carom, cricket, football, basketball etc.

Team mechnext is one of the six prominent technical clubs of J.C bose university of science and technology ymca eshtablished in January 2008. It is one of the core technical club's of YMCA.

We are the photography and designing society of JC Bose UST, established in 2012. Jhalak provides the platform to bring together the like minded people and share knowledge about the art etc.

Samarpan, an association of budding electronics and electrical engineers at YMCAUST was established by a group of people who share a common interest of inculcating and showcasing the tech-fire.
